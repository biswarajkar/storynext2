ESPN analyst: Colts' Jacoby Brissett 'will be a star'

Jacoby Brissett has surprised quite a few people in the media with the strong play that he has shown since taking over the starting quarterback job in Indianapolis. Due to the injury that will force Andrew Luck to miss the entire 2017 NFL season, Colts’ general manager Chris Ballard went out and made a trade with the New England Patriots to bring Brissett on board. Looking back on the trade, it is clear that the Colts made the right move. Brissett has blossomed in the starting role and has even made some question whether or not he could be a long-term option for the Colts.
